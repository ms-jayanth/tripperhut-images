Our Story

Where Every Journey
is a Masterpiece

Tripper Hut was born not in boardrooms, but on mountain roads and open skies — founded by brothers whose travels across India inspired thousands with cinematic visuals and authentic storytelling. Their vision: to turn passive viewers into active explorers.

What Sets Tripper Hut Apart:

Nature at the Core
Destinations handpicked for raw beauty — misty mountains, wild coasts, and hidden trails — ensuring every adventure is a sensory, cinematic experience.
Beyond Tourism
Trips dive deep into local culture, food, and stories, guided by the brothers' intimate knowledge of the land.
Community of Dreamers
More than travel packages, Tripper Hut curates journeys that unite a tribe of nature lovers and storytellers.
